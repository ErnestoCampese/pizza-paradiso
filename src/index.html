The Art of Biga

At Pizzeria Paradiso, we believe that a great pizza starts with great dough. That's why we use the traditional biga process, a time-honored technique that requires patience, skill, and the highest quality ingredients. By fermenting our dough for 48 hours, we achieve the perfect balance of flavor, texture, and digestibility, resulting in a pizza that is truly unforgettable.

Welcome to Paradiso

The art of pizza-making

Welcome to Pizzeria Paradiso, where we believe that great pizza is an art form. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be transported to a world of indulgent flavors, handcrafted with passion and care. Our traditional Neapolitan pizza recipe is the heart and soul of our menu, perfected over generations and brought to life by our master pizzaiolos.
